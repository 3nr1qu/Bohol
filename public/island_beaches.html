Bohol is the main island of Bohol Province, which also includes 75 smaller islands. This oval-shaped island is the tenth largest of the
Philippine archipelago. Bohol is a haven of tropical natural beauty. The coastline of the island
is skimmed by gentle coves and white sand beaches.
